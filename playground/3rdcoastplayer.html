Announcing the winners of the 2018 Third Coast/Richard H. Driehaus Competition!

The 2018 Third Coast/RHDF Competition award winners are here!

We won't announce "who won what" until the Third Coast Awards Ceremony, October 6th in Chicago - hosted by Tobin Low & Kathy Tu of Nancy Podcast ! Tobin & Kathy will reveal the winners of the following categories: Best Documentary (Gold, Silver, Bronze, Honorable Mention), Best Doc: Foreign Language, Best New Artist, Radio Impact, Best News Feature, Skylarking and - for the first time ever - Best Serialized Story. Learn more about these categories & their prizes!

And... listen to all the winning stories below (in alphabetical order, by title):
